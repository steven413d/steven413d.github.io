About Me (CV)

I got my M.S. degree at department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University supervised by Prof. Winston Hsu. Before coming to NTU, I got my B.Eng. degree in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, National Chiao Tung University in June 2016.

My research interest mainly lies in computer vision, deep learning, generative model, and human appearance editing. I am fortunate to work closely with Dr. Ziwei Liu (CUHK), Prof. Ping Luo (HKU) and Mr. Kaipeng Zhang (UTokyo).
