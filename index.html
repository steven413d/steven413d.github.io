About Me (CV)

I am a fourth-year PhD Candidate at department of ECE, UT Austin, advised by Prof. Alan Bovik. Before coming to UT Austin, I got my M.S. degree at department of CSIE, National Taiwa University supervised by Prof. Winston Hsu. I got my B.Eng. degree in the Department of ECE, National Chiao Tung University in June 2016.

My research interest mainly lies in Video Cropping, Video-language Modeling, and Image/Video Synthesis. Previously, I am fortunate to work with Prof. Min Sun (NTHU, Amazon) Prof. Ziwei Liu (NTU Singapore), Prof. Ping Luo (HKU) and Dr. Kaipeng Zhang (Shanghai AI Lab).

I am looking for a full-time position or an internship in 2026!
